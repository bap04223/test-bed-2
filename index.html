❓ How to Use the Training System

🚀 Getting Started

  1. Load Configurations: Enter your Chatbot ID and Scenario Sets ID, then click the load buttons to populate the dropdown menus.
  2. Select Chatbot: Choose the AI chatbot configuration you want to practice with from the dropdown.
  3. Select Scenario Set: Choose the collection of patient scenarios you want to use.
  4. Choose Sheet: Select the specific sheet containing your scenarios.
  5. Pick a Scenario: Either select a specific patient scenario or use "Random Scenario" for surprise practice.

🎤 Voice Controls

  1. Speech Recognition: Choose between Browser STT or Deepgram for speech-to-text conversion.
  2. Text-to-Speech: Select Browser TTS or ElevenLabs for AI patient voice responses.
  3. Recording: Press spacebar or click "Start Recording" to begin speaking.
  4. Review System: Your speech is transcribed and shown for review before sending.

💬 During the Consultation

  1. Start Recording: Press spacebar or click the microphone button to begin recording your message.
  2. Speak Naturally: Talk as you would with a real patient - introduce yourself, ask questions, provide advice.
  3. Review & Edit: Your speech will be transcribed and shown for review. Edit if needed before sending.
  4. Send Message: Click "Send Message" or press Enter to submit your consultation message.
  5. Listen to Response: The AI patient will respond with both text and voice using your selected TTS provider.

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Spacebar: Start/Stop recording (when not typing in text fields)
  • Enter: Send reviewed message (during review mode)
  • Escape: Close any open modal windows

📊 Managing Your Session

  • Clear Chat: Remove all messages to start fresh with the same scenario
  • New Session: Start completely over with a new session ID
  • Export Chat: Download your conversation as a text file for review
  • Submit Chat: Send your consultation to your instructor for feedback

🎯 Best Practices

  • Be Professional: Practice real-world communication skills with proper pharmacy consultation techniques
  • Ask Questions: Gather information about symptoms, medications, allergies, and medical history
  • Provide Clear Advice: Give specific, actionable recommendations with proper explanations
  • Show Empathy: Acknowledge patient concerns and demonstrate caring communication
  • Follow Up: Ask if the patient has any other questions or concerns

🔧 Troubleshooting

  • No Voice Recognition: Check microphone permissions and ensure you've selected a speech provider
  • No AI Response: Verify chatbot configuration is selected and scenario is loaded
  • Poor Speech Recognition: Speak clearly, reduce background noise, try switching to Deepgram if available
  • Can't Load Scenarios: Verify Google Sheet IDs are correct and sheets are publicly accessible
  • TTS Not Working: Check API keys for ElevenLabs or switch to Browser TTS

💡 Need More Help?

If you encounter issues or have suggestions for improvement, click the Feedback button to submit a detailed report. Include information about what you were trying to do and what went wrong for faster assistance.
